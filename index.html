CatGPT

Or: Statistically Modeling Feline Vocalization using Cosine Similarity of High Dimensional Vector Embeddings

About

CatGPT is a highly advanced statistic model of feline vocalization. It uses modern ML techniques to predict what a cat might say in response to any input. If CatGPT isn't responding to your input, perhaps you should give some thought about when cats meow.

The idea came to me in my sleep. No, I'm not joking. I genuinely woke up in the middle of the night and this was in my head. Special thanks to my friends at University for helping me iron out the details.
